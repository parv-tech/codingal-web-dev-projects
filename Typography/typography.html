What is cricket ?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball team sport played between two teams of 11 players each. It originated in England and is especially popular in countries like India, Australia, England, Pakistan, South Africa, and New Zealand .
Objective
Score more run than the opposing team .
how to score runs ?
1 team bats while the other balls and does feilding . The other team does the same after all the members of the team that batted FIRST are out .
