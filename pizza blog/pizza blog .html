Pizza blog

  • Pizza originated in Naples, Italy in the 18th or early 19th century.
  • Named after Queen Margherita of Savoy in 1889; its toppings (tomato, mozzarella, basil) represent the Italian flag.
  • Pepperoni is the most popular pizza topping in the United States.

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.
